# /dh lines

## Allows editing of Hologram lines

The `lines` command allows you to add, modify or remove Hologram lines.

## Permissions

-   `dh.command.lines`

See the [[Command Permissions|Permissions]] page for more details.

## Aliases

-   `line`
-   `l`

## Subcommands

-   ## [[/dh lines add|**/dh lines add**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Adds a line to a Hologram page
    
-   ## [[/dh lines addflag|**/dh lines addflag**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Adds a flag to a Hologram line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines align|**/dh lines align**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Aligns a Hologram line with another
    
-   ## [[/dh lines edit|**/dh lines edit**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Retrieve a pre-made command to edit a line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines height|**/dh lines height**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets the line height
    
-   ## [[/dh lines info|**/dh lines info**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Retrieves info about a Hologram Line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines insert|**/dh lines insert**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Inserts a line before others.
    
-   ## [[/dh lines offsetx|**/dh lines offsetx**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets the X offset of the line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines offsetz|**/dh lines offsetz**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets the Z offset of the line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines remove|**/dh lines remove**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Deletes a Hologram Line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines removeflag|**/dh lines removeflag**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Removes a Flag from a line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines set|**/dh lines set**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Set the content of a Line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines setfacing|**/dh lines setfacing**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets the facing angle of a Line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines setpermission|**/dh lines setpermission**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets a permission for a Line
    
-   ## [[/dh lines swap|**/dh lines swap**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Swaps two lines
