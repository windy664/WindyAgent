# /dh hologram

## Allows creation and modification of Holograms

The `hologram` command allows you to create, modify and remove Holograms.

## Permissions

-   `dh.command.hologram`

See the [[Command Permissions|Permissions]] page for more details.

## Aliases

-   `holo`
-   `h`

## Subcommands

-   ## [[/dh hologram addflag|**/dh hologram addflag**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Adds a Flag to a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram align|**/dh hologram align**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Aligns the Hologram with another one
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram center|**/dh hologram center**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Centers the hologram on a Block
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram clone|**/dh hologram clone**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Clones a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram create|**/dh hologram create**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Creates a new Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram delete|**/dh hologram delete**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Permanently deletes a hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram disable|**/dh hologram disable**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Disables a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram downorigin|**/dh hologram downorigin**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets the downorigin of a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram enable|**/dh hologram enable**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Enables a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram info|**/dh hologram info**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Displays general information about a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram lines|**/dh hologram lines**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Shows lines of a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram move|**/dh hologram move**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Teleports a Hologram to specific coordinates
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram movehere|**/dh hologram movehere**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Moves a Hologram to your current location
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram near|**/dh hologram near**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Lists all Holograms within a specific range
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram removeflag|**/dh hologram removeflag**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Removes a Flag from a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram rename|**/dh hologram rename**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Renames a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram setdisplayrange|**/dh hologram setdisplayrange**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets the max range a hologram should be visible from
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram setfacing|**/dh hologram setfacing**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets the direction in which heads and entities should look
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram setpermission|**/dh hologram setpermission**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets a permission required to see the hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram setupdateinterval|**/dh hologram setupdateinterval**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets the interval in which placeholders should update
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram setupdaterange|**/dh hologram setupdaterange**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Sets the max distance in which holograms update
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram teleport|**/dh hologram teleport**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Teleports you to a hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh hologram update|**/dh hologram update**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Forces a Hologram to update
