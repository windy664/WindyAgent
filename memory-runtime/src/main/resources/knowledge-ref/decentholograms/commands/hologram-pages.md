# /dh pages

## Allows the edit of Hologram pages

The `pages` command allows the adding, editing and deletion of Hologram pages.

## Permissions

-   `dh.command.pages`

See the [[Command Permissions|Permissions]] page for more details.

## Aliases

-   `page`
-   `p`

## Subcommands

-   ## [[/dh pages actions|**/dh pages actions**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Shows Click actions of a page
    
-   ## [[/dh pages add|**/dh pages add**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Adds a new Hologram Page
    
-   ## [[/dh pages addaction|**/dh pages addaction**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Adds an action to a Hologram Page
    
-   ## [[/dh pages clearactions|**/dh pages clearactions**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Removes all actions for a click type from a page
    
-   ## [[/dh pages insert|**/dh pages insert**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Insert a Page into a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh pages remove|**/dh pages remove**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Removes a page from a Hologram
    
-   ## [[/dh pages removeaction|**/dh pages removeaction**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Removes an Action from a Hologram Page
    
-   ## [[/dh pages swap|**/dh pages swap**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Swaps places of two Hologram pages
    
-   ## [[/dh pages switch|**/dh pages switch**]]
    
    * * *
    
    Switches to a different page (for another player)
